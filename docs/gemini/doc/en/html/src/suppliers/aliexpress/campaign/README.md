<h2>Steps</h2>

<ol>
  <li><b>Initialize Campaign Details:</b> Sets the campaign name, language, and currency.</li>
  <li><b>Create Campaign and Category Directories:</b> Creates the necessary directories for the campaign and its categories.</li>
  <li><b>Save Campaign Configuration:</b> Saves the campaign's configuration to a persistent storage.</li>
  <li><b>Collect Product Data:</b> Gathers the necessary product data for the campaign.</li>
  <li><b>Save Product Data:</b> Stores the collected product data.</li>
  <li><b>Create Promotional Materials:</b> Generates promotional materials for the campaign (e.g., images, descriptions).</li>
  <li><b>Review Campaign:</b> Allows for a comprehensive review of the campaign configuration and materials before publication.</li>
  <li><b>Is Campaign Ready?:</b> Checks if all aspects of the campaign are complete and valid.</li>
  <li><b>Publish Campaign:</b> Publishes the campaign for visibility on AliExpress.</li>
</ol>
